favourite places

Araku Valley is a hill station and valley region in the southeastern Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It's surrounded by the thick forests of the Eastern Ghats mountain range. The Tribal Museum is dedicated to the area's numerous indigenous tribes, known for their traditional Dhimsa dance, and showcases traditional handicrafts

Ramakrishna Beach also known as R K Beach is situated on the east coast of Bay of Bengal in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It is located near Dolphin's Nose. RK Beach gets its name from the Ramakrishna Mission ashram situated near the beach.

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is located amidst Kambalakonda Reserve Forest in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is the third largest zoo in the country. The zoological park is named after the former Prime Minister of India, Indira Gandhi. It was declared open to the public on 19 May 1977

favourote food

The taste of Manchurian cuisine can be described as rich, savory, and flavorful. It often features a balance of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors, with an emphasis on umami, the savory taste.

The taste of Chicken Dum Biryani is a harmonious blend of spices, fragrant rice, succulent chicken, and aromatic ingredients. It offers a combination of savory, spicy, and aromatic flavors that make it a beloved and satisfying dish for many.

Halwa is a popular dessert originating from the Indian subcontinent and the Middle East. It is made by cooking ingredients like semolina, wheat, carrots, lentils, or various fruits with ghee (clarified butter) and sugar, resulting in a rich, sweet, and aromatic dessert.
